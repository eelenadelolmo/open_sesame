The Executive of José María Aznar considers that the opening, by the National High Court, of an investigation to clarify whether there was any irregularity in the sale of the oil company Ertoil, a subsidiary of Ercros, to Cepsa, is the "second part " of the "campaign " that the PSOE initiated against the Minister of Industry, who, at the time of the sale, held an executive position in Ercros.
